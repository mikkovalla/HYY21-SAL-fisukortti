5.1	Change scenario: new feature 

Scenario: Adding new feature, AffiliateLinks, to the Fisukortti

Quality Attributes: Extensibility, maintainability 

Environment: Normal operation 

Stimulus: As popularity of the Fisukortti app rises, new affiliate partners want to do marketing through Fisukortti. 

Response: New feature will be implemented, because it is profitable and will increase visibility of the Fisukortti.

Description of system behavior: AffiliateLinks will be displayed at the Spot page. New microservice needs to be created for this change. 

Argumentation: Scenario is highly relevant, but easy to implement because of microservices architecture.

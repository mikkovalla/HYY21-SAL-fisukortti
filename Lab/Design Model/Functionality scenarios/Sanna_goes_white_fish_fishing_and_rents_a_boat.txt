Name: Sanna searches for a whitefish spot in Helsinki region, opens 2 Spots and registers to Skipperi.

Initial State: Fisukortti web service is active and whitefish season is open in March.

Actors: Sanna, Fisukortti

Steps:

1. Sanna opens Fisukortti web service in web browser.

2. Sanna inputs search terms 
    - FishingMethod: ''
    - Fish: 'Siika'
    - Place: 'Helsinki'

3. Fisukortti web service displays a list of Spots matching the search terms Sanna input.

4. Sanna clicks open the Spot listing for 'Hernesaari'.

5. Fisukortti renders the Spot for 'Hernesaari' in the browser.

6. Sanna scrolls through the Spot. 

7. Sanna closes the Spot.

8. Sanna clicks open the Spot listing for 'Uutela'.

9. Fisukortti renders the Spot for 'Uutela' in the browser.

10. Sanna scrolls through the Spot.

11. Sanna is interested in a boat rental from Skipperi displayed on Spot page.

12. Sanna clicks the Skipperi link and is taken to their web page to register as a customer.
